Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a CBZ file?

You can upload a CBZ file by dragging and dropping it onto the designated area on our homepage, or by clicking the "Select a File" / "Upload" button and choosing the file from your device.

What are the system requirements?

Comet Reader is a web-based PWA and works on most modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop and mobile devices. An internet connection is needed for initial loading and some features, though offline reading can be enabled for cached comics.

How does Manga Mode work?

Manga Mode reverses the page order and navigation controls to support the right-to-left reading format common in manga. You can toggle this in the reader settings.
